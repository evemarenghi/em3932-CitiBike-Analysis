This paper presents an analysis of CitiBike data. It tests the hypothesis that people in their 30s are less likely than people in their 20s to use CitiBike for commuting. I obtained CitiBike data, cleaned the data, created visualizations to describe the data, and performed a statistical test on the data. In order to correctly use a statistical test, I reformulated my null hypothesis slightly, which I will explain further in the conclusion. My results obtained via the Chi-Square test for proportions showed that there is an association between being in your 20s or being in your 30s and using CitiBike to com.
